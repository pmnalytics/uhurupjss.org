Uhuru Primary & Junior Secondary School

Public Basic Education Institution – Kenya

About the School

Uhuru Primary & Junior Secondary School is a public institution providing basic education under the Kenyan Competency Based Curriculum (CBC). The school focuses on academic excellence, character development, and holistic learner growth.
